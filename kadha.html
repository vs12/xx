PROCEDURE FOR MAKING AYURVEDIC KADHA

Ingredients :

1. Water: 2 cups
2. Ginger: 1â€³
3. Cloves: 4
4. Black Pepper: 5-6
5. Holy Basil (Tulsi): 5-6 leaves
6. Honey: 1/2tsp or to taste
7. Cinanmon stick(dalchini) (1 inch): 2

Procedure :

  1. To begin making ayurvedic kadha, add hot water in a pan.

  2. Crush ginger, cloves, black peppercorns and cinnamon.

  3. Add all the crushed ingredients along with basil leaves to the pan. Cook on medium heat for about 20 minutes or till the decoction is reduced to half.

  4. take the mixture in glass and drizzle few drops of honey before drinking.
